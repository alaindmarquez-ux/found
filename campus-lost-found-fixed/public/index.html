How CampusFind Works

A smarter way to recover lost items — built for students, by a student.

1

Report

Take a photo of the lost or found item and submit it with a brief description of where and when.

2

Store & Analyze

The system stores the photo and details, then runs keyword and category matching against existing reports.

3

Compare & Match

Potential matches are surfaced automatically based on category, color, brand, and description similarity.

4

Notify & Claim

Matched items are flagged so users can contact each other and arrange pickup on campus.

Why campus-only?

Only students with a verified account can post or claim items — keeping the community trusted and safe. No more sifting through a buried Facebook post or checking a dusty box in the admin hallway. CampusFind is purpose-built to get your stuff back faster.
